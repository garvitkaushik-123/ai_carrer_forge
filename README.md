# AI Career Forge

**Live App:** [ai-carrer-forge.vercel.app](https://ai-carrer-forge.vercel.app/)

## What is AI Career Forge?

AI Career Forge is an AI-powered interview readiness tool that helps job seekers understand how prepared they are for their next interview — and exactly what to do to improve.

## How It Works

1. **Upload Your Resume** — Upload your resume (PDF or DOCX) and the AI analyzes it to understand your skills, experience, and target role.

2. **Answer a Few Questions** — Based on your resume, the app generates personalized questions covering your technical skills, communication ability, and portfolio. These are a mix of multiple choice and short answer.

3. **Get Your Score** — The AI evaluates your responses across four dimensions:
   - **Technical Skills** — Do you have the right skills for your target role?
   - **Resume Quality** — Is your resume well-structured, ATS-friendly, and impactful?
   - **Communication** — Can you articulate your experience clearly and professionally?
   - **Portfolio** — Do you have projects and work samples that demonstrate your abilities?

4. **Actionable Feedback** — Each dimension comes with:
   - Your strengths (what's already working)
   - Specific areas to improve (with concrete action steps)
   - Topics you should study next
   - Links to recommended articles, courses, and resources

## Who Is This For?

- Students preparing for their first job interviews
- Professionals switching careers who want to identify gaps
- Anyone who wants honest, specific feedback on their interview readiness before applying

## Built With

AI Career Forge uses Google Gemini AI to analyze resumes and evaluate responses, providing personalized and actionable career guidance.
